Which Matatu ?

The journey begins

Here you'll find the answer to the question which matatu do I take to...?

The process is simple

Go to the search button and enter your destination.

The application will show you matatus that drop off within a 2km radius of your destination

The matatu closest to you will be displayed in a different color.

Click on a matatu in the map to see it's other drop off points and some more information on it.
